Tokenonmics Update: January 29 2026







Crypto Risk Premia

Crypto Pricing with Hidden Factors posted to arXiv

  • Market premium is much larger with latent factors: 24.5% annualized using the latent factor vs 8.5% from standard methods => unobserved factors matter
  • Size effect is “backwards” in crypto: -70% annualized => investors strongly prefer larger cryptocurrencies
  • Evidence of equity–crypto integration: Software industry, plus broad equity market and profitability (RMW) components. Evidence of overlapping investor bases and shared “risk-on/risk-off” channels.

New-to-literature state variables

  • Fear & Greed shocks show priced effects (sentiment matters). Supports TVL/X multisort results.
  • Altcoin Season Index is largely a proxy for broader latent risk
  • Hacks not priced

TVL:

  • TVL looks weakly priced under Fama–MacBeth, but becomes insignificant under latent-factor controls
  • TVL mainly proxying for broader latent/common risk rather than carrying an independent price of risk

In sum:

  • Model risk is nontrivial—whether you control for latent factors can flip the size/magnitude/significance of premia
  • Risk management/hedging should consider tech/profitability equity channels alongside crypto-native factors
  • Sentiment measures can improve monitoring of expected-return regimes, but some popular “rotation” proxies may not be incremental once common risks are captured
